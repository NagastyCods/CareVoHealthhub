About CareVo Health Hub

Who We Are

CareVo Health is a student-focused health screening service designed to improve early detection of diseases and promote preventive healthcare on university campuses.

Professional Coordination

All services are coordinated by trained Medical Laboratory Scientists and conducted through licensed partner laboratories to ensure accuracy, safety, and regulatory compliance.

Our Mission

To make reliable health screening accessible, affordable, and student-friendly while maintaining high ethical and professional standards in laboratory testing and result interpretation.

Core Values

  • Accuracy and quality assurance
  • Confidentiality and data protection
  • Ethical testing practices
  • Student-centered healthcare delivery
  • Preventive health education
